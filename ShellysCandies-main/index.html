SHELLY'S HOMEMADE TOFFEE CANDY (since 1986)

A love for cooking for her family...

Michelle began cooking for others when she married in 1983. Taking care of her husband was just the start of her baking journey. Soon, they had 2 kids. Then 3, and eventually 5 little rugrats were running around. A lot was changing in Shelly's life — but one thing that has remained constant to this day is her simple yet delicious recipe for her homemade toffee that has become a highly coveted treat at many family gatherings. Her children look forward to it every year, and now so do her customers!

Shelly's candy was a hit at the "Chocolate Lover's Affair" event in Pocatello, Idaho in 2023, where she gave away free samples and sold over 10 lb of delicious homemade toffee.

Holidays are better with Shelly's Toffee Candy!

Bring home Shelly's divine toffee candy this holiday season and you'll see why her kids couldn't go without it at Christmastime. Simply crafted with the best ingredients, it's a delicious treat that you won't be able to eat just once!
